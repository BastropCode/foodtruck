From The Lost Pines

Lost Pines Food Truck started as a weekend cookout under the tall pines outside Bastrop. We built our menu around slow smoke, bright Texas spice, and the kind of hospitality you feel on a front porch.

Every plate is made with local ingredients, hand-mixed rubs, and a little campfire soul. Pull up a chair, grab a taco, and stay a while.
